ABOUT MY SELF?

makes Senior High School interesting? The answer is,the knowledge that the teachers share to us, bond between students and
teachers, bonds between student that will end up to being friends and also sharing ideas and knowledge between the students.
Ofcourse there are also ups and downs, getting low remarks on exams or quiz, having misunderstanding with friends.
